Food and Culture in Bangalore

Introduction

The bustling city of Bangalore located in the South of India, and at the heart of Karnataka, is a city known for its shopping as well as food culture. Being the shopping capital of India, Bangalore is a hub for foodies all around the world to enjoy the variety of cuisines available to relish. In this visualisation we seek to explore some of Bangalore's resturants and it's food culture. We also want to see how the demographics of the city looks like.

This map above, showcases the spread of a sample of resturants across the city as a dot. The color of the dot represents the resturants main cuisine and the size reprsents its rating. Are Resturants really worth the pricey charge?

Interestingly,we find that the best resturants in Bangalore are often the simplest and in fact cheapest! Here, we discover that the idea 'the more you pay, the better the quality' doesn't exist in the city that has over 50,000 resturants! Another fascinating analysis is that chain food organisations like 'Pizza hut' and 'Onesta' are overpriced for the quality they serve.
