Food and Culture in Bangalore

Where are Bangalorians situated around resturants?

The bustling city of Bangalore located in the South of India, and at the heart of Karnataka, is a city known for its shopping as well as food culture. Being the shopping capital of India, Bangalore is a hub for foodies all around the world to enjoy the variety of cuisines available to relish. In this visualisation we seek to explore some of Bangalore's resturants and it's food culture
